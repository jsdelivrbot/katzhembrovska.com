Hey there! I’m Kat, an aspiring industrial designer with a strong interest in human factors, ergonomics, and research. I enjoy finding solutions to improve the day-to-day lives of people and exploring traditional manufacturing processes.
I’m currently a student at California College of the Arts where I am pursuing a double major in industrial design and illustration, with a minor in Writing and literature (expected grad date May 2020). At CCA I have had the opportunity to design for a wide range of users which aids in my development of empathy and identifying key pain points. After college, my long-term goal is to work in medical design and develop post-operative assistive devices.


I am currently seeking internship opportunities in the Bay Area that revolve around human centered design, ergonomics, and research.
